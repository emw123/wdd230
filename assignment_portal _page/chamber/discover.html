Discover all the Things Puyallup Has to Offer!

Mount Rainier is one of the most stunning aspects of Puyallup. Visable from many different places in the city, the mountain offers great views and wonderful activties such as hiking and rockclimbing.
The Puyallup Fair is a very old part of Puyallup's history. Open once a year, this fair brings in many parts of the Puyallup culture, and also has many fun rides and attractions.
Puyallup and the surrounding areas have many beautiful forest, and walking trails are very common. It's a great place to get in contact with nature!
The Museum of Glass showcases many different glass sculptures, as well as giving live examples of how they are made. It's a wonderful educating experience.
Puyallup is home to many encanting tulip fields, where it is often possible to select and buy a few tulips,
Snowshoeing is a common activity that takes place a Mount Rainier. It allows exploration even when there is snow on the ground.
If you're not interested in driving to Seattle, the Puget Sound Ferry will get you and your car there in no time!
Puyallup is home to many wonderful parks varying from pretty nature walks to playgrounds for kids. They're great places to spend a summer afternoon.
The wildflowers next to Mount Rainier are a beautiful sight, and a good place to take a rest after hiking down the mountain.
