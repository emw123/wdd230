Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide information to patrons seeking to find a place to stay while attending the temple. It is also intended to be where reservations can be made for these accommodations.

Target Audience

The target audience is meant to be people who are attending the temple, and who may live far enough away that an overnight stay may be necessary. It is also meant for temple workers, and any family members of either temple workers or patrons.
